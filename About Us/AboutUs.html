About Us

New to foodicted? Welcome!
We are a team working to make people's life a bit more easier by updating you all with new homemade recipes!









What is Foodicted?

Foodicted is a small food recipe website, which contains many innovative food recipes
which also enchances your daily routine with our easy-cheatsheet of recipes.
We have 50+ recipes ranging from vegan to non-vegetarian recipes.
We launched this project on 1st July 2021.

About our Project

Foodicted is a college-level project built by a team of 3 undergraduates from K J Somaiya college of Engineering.
We used multiple Web-Development technologies for building our project.
We used HTML, CSS, JavaScript for the FrontEnd and Backend as well as per Requirement.
